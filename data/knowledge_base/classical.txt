Classical music encompasses orchestral, chamber, and solo instrumental works built on formal compositional structures such as sonatas, symphonies, and concertos. The sound is acoustic and dynamic, ranging from delicate solo piano to the full power of a symphony orchestra. Energy and mood shift dramatically within a single piece — from serene and contemplative to triumphant and emotionally overwhelming. The absence of lyrics places the expressive weight entirely on melody, harmony, rhythm, and orchestration. Typical listeners include intellectually curious adults, musicians, and anyone seeking an immersive, emotionally complex listening experience.
