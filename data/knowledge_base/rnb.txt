R&B (Rhythm and Blues) blends smooth, soulful vocals with groove-based production rooted in funk, soul, and contemporary electronic beats. The energy is sensual and fluid — less about raw power and more about feel, phrasing, and vocal runs that convey deep emotion. Moods span romantic and intimate to empowering and self-assured, with lyrics centering on love, desire, heartbreak, and personal resilience. Modern R&B incorporates trap hi-hats, lush synths, and minimalist bass lines beneath expressive lead vocals. Typical listeners are young adults who connect with emotional authenticity and value musicianship alongside contemporary production.
