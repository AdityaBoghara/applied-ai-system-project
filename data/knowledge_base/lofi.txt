Lo-fi music features a deliberately low-fidelity aesthetic with soft beats, vinyl crackle, and warm, slightly muffled tones that evoke a nostalgic, lived-in feeling. The energy is consistently low — slow tempos, minimal arrangement, and no sudden dynamic shifts make it ideal background music. The mood is calm, introspective, and cozy, often described as melancholic yet soothing. Samples from jazz, soul, and anime soundtracks are frequently layered beneath gentle hip-hop rhythms. Typical listeners are students, remote workers, and creatives who use it to focus, relax, or wind down without the distraction of lyrics.
