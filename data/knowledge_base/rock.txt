Rock music is driven by electric guitars, a strong backbeat, and powerful vocals that convey intensity and attitude. Energy levels vary from the anthemic drive of classic rock to the raw aggression of hard rock and punk. The mood tends toward passionate, rebellious, or introspective, exploring themes of freedom, struggle, and identity. Guitar solos, distortion, and live-sounding production give rock its visceral, high-impact character. Typical listeners are people who value authenticity and emotional directness — often in their teens through middle age, spanning casual fans to dedicated enthusiasts.
