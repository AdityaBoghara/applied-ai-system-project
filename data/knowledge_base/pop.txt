Pop music is defined by its polished, radio-friendly sound built around catchy hooks, melodic choruses, and repetitive song structures. The energy is upbeat and danceable, often featuring synthesizers, drum machines, and layered vocals. The mood ranges from euphoric and celebratory to bittersweet and romantic. Lyrics focus on universal themes like love, friendship, and personal empowerment. Typical listeners are broad and mainstream — teens and young adults who want feel-good, accessible music for social settings and everyday life.
