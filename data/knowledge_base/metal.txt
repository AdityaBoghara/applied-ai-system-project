Metal is characterized by heavily distorted guitars, fast and precise drumming, powerful bass lines, and vocals that range from operatic to aggressive screaming. The energy is extremely high — relentless tempos, complex rhythms, and wall-of-sound production create an intense, overwhelming listening experience. The mood ranges from dark and menacing to cathartic and empowering, exploring themes of power, darkness, mythology, and personal struggle. Subgenres like death metal, thrash, and doom extend the spectrum further in terms of speed, heaviness, and emotional tone. Typical listeners are passionate and community-driven — often teens and adults who find catharsis and belonging in metal's intensity and technical complexity.
